Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PlanetX ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our AI career growth SaaS at www.genz.ltd and related applications that connect to our API at api.genz.ltd.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: email address, authentication identifiers, and subscription tier.
  • Career content: resumes, job preferences, quiz responses, and messages you submit to AI features.
  • Usage data: feature usage, timestamps, device/browser metadata, and diagnostic logs.
  • Payment data: billing status and transaction references. Card details are processed by Stripe; we do not store full payment card numbers.

2. How we use information

  • Provide and improve AI coaching, resume parsing, and job matching services.
  • Authenticate users, enforce plan limits, and prevent abuse.
  • Process subscriptions, invoices, and support requests.
  • Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Legal bases (where applicable)

Depending on your region, we rely on contract performance, legitimate interests, consent (where required), and legal compliance to process personal data.

4. Sharing and processors

We may share data with service providers that help us operate PlanetX, including:

  • Cloud hosting and database providers
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Email and customer support tools

We do not sell personal information.

5. International transfers

Data may be processed in jurisdictions where our infrastructure or subprocessors operate. We apply appropriate safeguards where required by law.

6. Retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion subject to applicable law.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data, and to object to certain processing.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures such as HTTPS, access controls, and monitoring. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Children

PlanetX is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their data.
