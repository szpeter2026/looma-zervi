Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

This policy explains how to cancel PlanetX subscriptions and when refunds may be available. PlanetX is a digital SaaS service billed monthly in USD.

1. How to cancel

You can cancel your subscription by:

  • Using the account billing settings in the PlanetX application (when available), or
  • Emailing support@genz.ltd from your registered email with subject "Cancel subscription".

Cancellation stops future renewals. It does not delete your account unless you also request account deletion.

2. Effect of cancellation

  • Your paid features remain available until the end of the current billing period.
  • After the period ends, your account reverts to the Free plan limits unless you resubscribe.
  • No further charges will be made after a successful cancellation before the renewal date.

3. Refund policy

Because PlanetX delivers digital services immediately upon purchase, fees are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started.

We may provide a refund at our discretion when:

  • You were charged after a confirmed cancellation due to a processing error
  • There was a duplicate charge or technical billing mistake
  • Required by applicable consumer protection law

4. Free trials and promotions

If a promotional trial is offered, trial terms will be shown at signup. Unless stated otherwise, converting to a paid plan begins billing at the displayed USD monthly price. Cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges.

5. Chargebacks and disputes

Please contact support@genz.ltd before initiating a chargeback so we can investigate billing issues quickly.

6. Enterprise customers

Custom contracts may include different cancellation and refund terms as specified in the signed agreement.

7. Contact

Billing support: support@genz.ltd
